VikiDesign Art

This page is dedicated to showcase the artwotk of Viktoria Hristova, a passionate artist and designer.

  • Stag In A Forest

    • This print portrays a beautiful stag in a forest, looking out towards the viewer. The nature is exposed in numerous colors, bringing out complexity and pulse of the forest, showing a little glimpse of its spirit. This peaceful setting seems undisturbed and quiet, only for the singing of the birds and buzzing of the bees. The viewer is brought to a place that is sacred, and can be left only to admire the beauty of it all.

  • Puffin Kingdom

    • This original fine art print is a beautiful set of vibrant colors flowing together harmoniously to create an exotic scene. The balance between abstract and realistic forms makes an interesting dynamic and keeps the eyes wondering, while the cool colors provide a calming effect. The Puffin's beautiful beak is accented in red with similar colors popping in the foreground.

  • Majestic Peacock

    • This original fine art print shows the beautiful peacock tail in detail. The feathers flow together, like a jewel, creating a colorful web for the viewer to admire. The bird stands tall, showing off its majestic features with an abstract set of lines woven together. The vibrant colors bring joy in any setting, making this print the perfect gift for anyone.
